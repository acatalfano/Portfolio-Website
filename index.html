WIREFRAMES

These are some portions of the wireframes I've made in planning my websites. Wireframes are critical to my design process—they show how the design will roughly look and suggest how it can potentially feel

PROTOTYPES

I next turn my wireframe into an interactive prototype. This is the point where the design's "feel" matures and it can more clearly be demonstrated what a typical user-interaction would be like

FINAL PRODUCTS

It had to happen sometime and there's no time like the present! My next step is to develop my prototypes into the actual final product. This is where the functionality recieves less attention (because it had darn well better be figured out at this point) and the aesthetics steal the spotlight. Perhaps most noticably, this is where color is finally added!
